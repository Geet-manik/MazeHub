Tree 1
The city in which Prabhav lived had many thieves. He has a cycle that his father gifted him on his birthday and which is very precious to him. To prevent his cycle from getting stolen, he goes to the market to buy a circular chain and a lock but Unfortunately, all the circular chains were sold 10 minutes before his arrival. Luckily he got a broken chain in the garden. There were 4 chain pieces with 3 loops each. He can cut any loop and join it to any other loop. Prabhav has some pain in his right arm so he wants to make the circular chain with minimal effort. How many minimum number of cuts will be required to make a circular chain?
